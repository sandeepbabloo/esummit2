What is E-Summit?

Entrpreneurship

One of Asia’s largest business event , E-Summit is the flagship event conducted by ecell iitb. It’s a conclave of competitions, workshops, panel discussions and Speeches from the biggest names in the world of Biz. Starting from a humble beginning as a fledgling idea, it’s surreal to see it’s phenomenal rise into the towering edifice it is today, fourteen years later. Join us for three memorable days of immersing yourself into the great grand world of entrepreneurship on 19th and 20th January 2019 at IIT Bombay. Be there, for words can only describe so little.

Why Paradigm of Disruption?

E-Summit'19

The essence of business relies on it’s inherent dynamic behavior. We, at ecell iitb believe that this capricious nature of business must not be confined to the shackles of a fixed set of rules. This year , we set this beast free , and hope to radicalize schools of thought preaching a ‘formulaic’ approach to Business. Heralding this mercurial idea, we follow the only protocol of a total overhaul of existing ‘routes’. This tradition espoused by Ecell , manifested itself, into the theme of E-Summit ‘19 , “A Paradigm of Disruption”.
